Who We Are

A Movement for a Greener Future

Mbogi Green Mashinani is a youth-led community-based organization dedicated to environmental conservation through active participation in reforestation, climate action advocacy, and promoting sustainable practices in Uasin Gishu County, Kenya.

Making an Impact, One Tree at a Time

From local schools to entire communities, our projects are designed to create lasting environmental change.
